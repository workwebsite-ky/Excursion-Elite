Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for standard packages and 6–12 months for ultra-exclusive or peak-season trips. However, our team is experienced at last-minute luxury arrangements too.

Are packages fully customizable? +

Absolutely. Every Excursion Elite journey is built from scratch around your preferences, schedule, and vision. Our curated packages serve as a starting point, but nothing is fixed.

What's included in a package? +

Our packages typically include private transfers, luxury accommodations, guided experiences, select dining, and 24/7 concierge support. Flights can be included or arranged separately based on preference.

Do you handle group and corporate travel? +

Yes. We specialize in corporate retreats, incentive travel programs, and private group expeditions of all sizes — from intimate gatherings to large-scale corporate events in extraordinary settings.

Is there a cancellation or refund policy? +

We offer flexible cancellation terms depending on the package and notice period. Our team will clearly outline all terms before you confirm, and we always recommend comprehensive travel insurance.
